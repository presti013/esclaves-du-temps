I. SOCHI: THE FOUNDING LIE
On the night of October 23–24, 2024, the Russo-American regime did not suffer a terrorist attack. It perpetrated one. Four hundred and twelve people were murdered at the Winter Theatre of Sochi by a zone chronophage weapon — aged seventy years in three and a half seconds. Among them: twenty-eight oligarchs too independent, two generals too idealistic, and three hundred and eighty-two collateral victims whose only crime was to be present.
The Vesna movement never possessed this technology. The televised “confessions” were extracted under torture. The “miracle survivor” Aleksandr Mikhailov died of a “heart attack” twelve hours after his scripted testimony. The CIA provided a fifteen-minute SIGINT blackout — the complicit silence that let the FSB disguise a purge as a national tragedy.
Sochi was not an act of terrorism. It was a founding act. The first stone of the temple where humanity would be sacrificed on the altar of temporal profit.
We refuse to forget. We refuse to forgive. And we refuse, categorically, to live in the world that Sochi legitimized.
II. THE PATTERN THAT REPEATS
History does not repeat itself, but it rhymes. Every great scientific breakthrough follows the same arc: conceived to liberate, hijacked to dominate, privatized to exploit.
1938 — Nuclear fission. The dream: clean energy for all. Six years later: Hiroshima. 1991 — World Wide Web. Tim Berners-Lee places it in the public domain. Thirty years later: surveillance capitalism, every click monetized. 2012 — CRISPR. The promise: eradicate genetic disease. The result: genetic apartheid. 2024 — Temporal compression. Kenji Nomura develops the theory, Jarek Mironen applies it to decontaminate soil. Ten years later: three million time slaves.
The pattern is not a bug. It is the core function.
III. WHAT THE SYSTEM DEMANDS
Profit demands the concentration of power. A genuinely liberating technology threatens the economic order. It must be captured, patented, privatized. National security demands a monopoly on violence. Any transformative technology becomes a military concern. Social control demands surveillance. Every network becomes a monitoring tool.
CT Corp did not corrupt Mironen’s technology. It revealed its inevitable fate under this system. The system must be destroyed. Not reformed. Destroyed.
IV. OUR COMMITMENT
We are not terrorists. We reject all blind violence. But neither are we naive pacifists. We are ready to sacrifice our comfort, our safety, our careers. We are ready to sacrifice our time. Many of us volunteer to infiltrate the complexes, accepting premature aging to sabotage the system from within.
We are ready to sacrifice our lives.
Water always finds a way.
— Malek Veyran
