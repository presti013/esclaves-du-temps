Original Soundtrack

A novel is read. This one is heard, too.

Slaves of Time was composed with its own score, written by Laurent Prestigiacomo chapter by chapter. Each track is not an illustration — it is an additional layer of the text, to lay over your ears before you open the book, while you read, or after, when the characters refuse to leave you.

Thirty-three tracks. A single universe.
